# IR
A collection of freely available Impulse Responses that I use, tracked because they are hard to find and organize   

Trying to preserve original links in this README   

# List and explanations
### Dauntless IR pack
Made by [resington](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1GjnDMSXQTE)  
Numbered 1-10, higher number = more aggressive sounding.  
Res Dauntless X - custom mix
"this pack is free to share however you wish but is not for resale"

### 1960a
No idea where it came from, downloaded for free in ~2023?  
A lot of IRs in this folder

### IOWA Slipknot
Made by [Taylor Danley](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=itm_Xb-PGGU)   
1999 Carvin cabinet that was used to record IOWA

### Master Of Puppets
Made by [Taylor Danley](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=i1Czg5hsotY) deconstructing Metallica's tone  
IR for the Marshall cabinet he used in the video

### Shift Line Bass IR
Supposedly [this pack](https://shift-line.com/irpackbass) by shift-line.com

### DYNAX
These are free low quality samples for 22$ full [IR packs](https://dynax-japan.com/en/sale/ir/) (sold per-cabinet)   
Split into folders and renamed for ease of use (DYNAX MaseBoo RECTI212 SampleA -> DY MaseBoo RECTI212 A)  
Most cab names are altered, but you can look the original specs up on their [page](https://dynax-japan.com/en/sale/ir/)

### ML Sound Lab 2025 Free IR
Free IR calibration pack from ML sound lab. Not sure if it's that usable, but it still works.   
Not a traditional IR, has some separate amp color tones and what not: check the PDF for details

### Allure pack
https://line6.com/allure/   
Old free IR pack from line6. just some cool cabs

### Free Impulse Responses Modern Metal
https://modernmusicsolutions.com/blogs/free/free-ir

### FREE BASS IR Pack
https://modernmusicsolutions.com/blogs/free/free-ir

### SMG UK V30
Free IRs from Glenn Fricker's (https://www.youtube.com/@SpectreSoundStudios) [mailing list](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=luPepJlFQCI)
